The Legacy

Bridging Tech with Culture

Welcome to INNOVISION 2026, the premium flagship national technical and cultural college festival hosted by Apex State University. Spanning three incredible days, our fest serves as a melting pot of pure academic curiosity and boundless creative expression.

Whether you are a coder eager to dominate hackathons, an aspiring roboticist designing autonomous machines, or an artist waiting to light up the stage, Innovision provides an unparalleled competitive stage and a celebration of youthful spirit.

Our Vision

To inspire a generation of students to design state-of-the-art technological solutions that address urgent human concerns.

Our Mission

To foster community learning, healthy artistic rivalries, and provide direct connection to technological leaders.

FAQ

Common Queries

Got questions about tickets, food allocations, accommodation, or event rule checks? Find responses below.

Who is eligible to register for Innovision 2026?

Any active student with a valid university identity card (undergraduate, post-graduate, or diploma students) is fully eligible to participate in all our cultural and technical contests.

Can I register for multiple events across days?

Yes, absolutely! However, we highly recommend checking the Day-wise chronological timings on our Schedule page to ensure none of your registered events overlap.

Is safe student accommodation provided?

Yes, shared accommodation with food allowances inside the university student hostels will be allocated to out-of-station participants registering before March 5, 2026.

How are registration fees handled?

Most of our technical challenges are completely free to enter. Performing cultural events may require a small processing fee per team, details of which are emailed upon initial registration.
